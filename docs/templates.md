# Templates

## `default`

Use it when you want a clean Nuxt 4 starting point without locking into a specific product shape.

Best for:

- general product apps
- prototypes
- small to mid-size projects
- teams that want a neutral foundation

## `dashboard`

Use it for internal products, admin panels, and operational tooling.

Best for:

- admin dashboards
- internal tools
- analytics panels
- back-office apps

## `landing`

Use it when the first priority is presentation and conversion.

Best for:

- marketing sites
- launch pages
- company sites
- product showcases

## `saas`

Use it for product-first sites with a stronger SaaS feel out of the box.

Best for:

- SaaS home pages
- subscription products
- startup sites
- product marketing with application routes later

## Picking a Template

- Choose `default` if you want maximum flexibility.
- Choose `dashboard` if the app is used after login.
- Choose `landing` if the homepage is the product.
- Choose `saas` if the product and marketing site share the same shell.
